A ble beacon enabled
scavenger hunt

How it works

When the game is started, a timer will begin, and clue cards will appear.

Search

Try to find the object the clues describe. When you have found the correct object, your phone will be alerted by the object's beacon.

Stumped?

If you can't find the object from its first clue, you can request two more clues. However, the more clues you request, the less points you get!

Winning

The goal is to find all objects using the least amount of clues, in the shortest time possible.

Submit

When you are done, submit your score. The scores will be ranked and the winner will be announced!

Ready?

Clicking the start button will begin the timer, so wait until you are ready to start the game before pressing it!
